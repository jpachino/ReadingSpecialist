About Our Reading Specialist

Nechuma Schweitzer holds a master’s degree in special education and brings over 12 years of teaching experience to her practice. As a certified Associate Orton-Gillingham reading specialist through the Orton-Gillingham Academy, she has completed more than 150 hours of specialized training in the field and has helped countless students master reading using this proven, multisensory approach.

Nechuma’s passion extends beyond teaching foundational skills; she is dedicated to fostering her students’ confidence and instilling a lifelong love for reading. Her lessons are filled with joy, energy, and creativity, allowing her to connect deeply with each student. By creating a positive and supportive environment, she encourages cooperation, enthusiasm, and a sense of accomplishment that inspires students to reach their fullest potential.

With her expertise, our reading specialist uses a variety of evidence-based techniques to help each learner progress at their own pace, ensuring that every individual reaches their full potential.
